Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and keep all collected data secure and local. Here's what we store:

  • Message Logs: Messages from public channels are logged for stat tracking.
  • Voice Activity: We log voice session duration to show time spent in voice chats.
  • DMs are never logged or read.
  • All data is stored in a private SQLite database accessible only by the server admin or bot owner.

We do not share or sell any data to third parties.
